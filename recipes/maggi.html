Maggi

Description

Maggi is an instant ramen noodle company based out of Sweden. Their signature product is also called Maggi noodles. This recipe is a quick and simple method of making maggi with a little twist added to it.

Ingredients

  • Maggi Noodles
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Cabbage
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chili Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Oil

Directions

  1. Boil water in a pot and add salt and oil
  2. Put the Maggi cakes without breaking them
  3. Let Maggi cook (undercook a little bit)
  4. Once ready, strain out the Maggi and rinse in cold water
  5. Chop onions, and grate the carrots and cabbage
  6. In a pan heat oil
  7. Once warm, cook onions until they are translucent
  8. Add in carrots and cabbage and cook until cabbage is translucent
  9. Reduce heat and add tastemaker, salt, pepper, soy sauce, vinegar, and chili sauce
  10. Add in noodles and cook on low heat for 5 min
  11. Serve
